For Employers

If a Candidate is identified through EmpowerXx and accepts a Job Offer within six months of the Candidate's EmpowerXx profile being visible to the Employer, you as the Employer will be charged a Placement Fee, regardless of whether the Candidate accepts an Interview Request. The Placement Fee (excluding VAT) is equal to 12.5% of the Candidate's annual total CTC, inclusive of all guaranteed income. EmpowerXx will invoice you within one week of the Candidate's Start Date and the Placement Fee shall be due 30 days after the Start Date. If you already had an Active Process with the Candidate, and complete the process on EmpowerXx, then a Placement Fee will be due. An Active Process means that there was at least one communication regarding a potential hiring (email, call, interview, ...) between the Candidate and the Employer at least 90 days before the Candidate's profile was made visible on the EmpowerXx platform.
